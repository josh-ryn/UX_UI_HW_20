Goal

Develop an app that offers itinerary collaboration and sharing features for group travel. Possible additions include a voting tool for group input on activities/ attractions.
*

Research Questions

- How can we make this application a primary tool for sharing/ collaborating itineraries?
- What type of users will use this app?
- How can this product be used for more than just itinerary sharing?
- How does having access to an app of this sort benefit prospective users?
*

Methodology

- Two separate dates will be used for User Interviews. These interviews will be conducted to collect qualitative data from participants and will be used to craft a user persona.
*

Participants

The ideal participant will consist of people who regularly travel with groups. Our primary targets:
- Will often be the primary planners for the group.
- Are often the main communicators who provide options for the group to vote on.
- Communicate deadlines and/or provide the finalized itinerary.
*

Conduct Live User Interviews

A series of interview questions have been compiled to help craft a user persona and provide fresh, first-hand insight.

Problem Statement

Travelers planning for groups need a user-friendly interface for planning and productivity. Alternative options aren't easily implemented into group settings which is causing users to revert to familiar interfaces.
